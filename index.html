Overpowered Main Characters in Isekai Manga

The trend...

Isekai is a Japanese genre that describes stories in which individuals are transported to a different world to accomplish a certain goal.

It has become a common resouce in these stories that the main protagonist(s) become extremely powerful, often to absurd levels that people consider "overpowered" in the alternate reality. However, there is always some explanation for why this happens.

Genre Elements

World Types

  • Virtual Reality
  • Fantasical World
  • Past of Future Timeline
  • Parallel Universe
  • Reincarnation

Common Objectives

  • Surviving
  • Finding a way back
  • Saving the Alternate Reality
  • Beating a Game / Challenge

Sword Art Online (SAO)

Plot

This story takes place in Aincrad, a virtual fantasy world where players' minds were transported through a neural device, the NerveGear. On the release day, it was announced that players would not be able to log out, and dying in the game would also kill them in real life. To escape they must beat 100 floors full of countless enemies and obstacles.

The Fastest Swordman

Kirito is the main character. He is one of the few beta testers who had the chance to play the video game before its release. Bringing all the information he gained from previously testing the game made him incredibly powerful in Aincrad. Kirito knew many secrets to beat each floor and progress ridiculously fast in the game.

I'm Standing on a Million Lives

Plot

A group of 3 high schoolers is transported to a series of games where they are prompted to complete missions by an unknown being. It is later revealed that their missions take place in alternate worlds, striking the morality of the main characters since they killed many people during those missions.

Morality

The three protagonists will respawn if they die during their missions as long as one of them stays alive. Since they are almost invincible, the story loses focus on whether they will succeed or not. Instead, it takes the approach of giving a moral lesson, depicting how regular people would react if placed into the role of a main character in a fantasy world.

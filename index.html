The Genre (Action ~ Shonen)

Superheroes vs Villains, Magical Battles, Martial Arts...
Even though the possibilities when writing an Action story are countless, people get into an Action story looking for clashes of strength between characters.

The trend...

Action comics heavily rely on demonstrations of power, but some Japanese comics take this to a ridiculous scale, making the protagonists of their stories almost invincible.
